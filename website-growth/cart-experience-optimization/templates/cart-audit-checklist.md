# Cart Experience Audit Checklist

Use this checklist to identify friction points in your current cart experience
and prioritize optimizations to increase your Checkout Start Rate.

## 1. Transparency & Costs
- [ ] **Shipping Visibility:** Are shipping costs shown or estimated in the cart?
- [ ] **Free Shipping Threshold:** Is there a clear indicator of how much more
      is needed for free shipping?
- [ ] **Tax Estimation:** Is there an estimated tax or a clear "Tax calculated
      at checkout" message?
- [ ] **Total Accuracy:** Is the final total (Subtotal + Shipping + Tax)
      presented as clearly as possible?

## 2. Layout & Primary Action
- [ ] **CTA Contrast:** Does the "Checkout" button have the highest visual
      weight on the page?
- [ ] **Mobile Stickiness:** Is the "Checkout" button sticky at the bottom of the
      viewport on mobile?
- [ ] **Express Payments:** Are Apple Pay / PayPal / Shop Pay options available
      directly in the cart?
- [ ] **Above the Fold:** On desktop, is the order summary and "Checkout" button
      visible without scrolling?

## 3. Interaction & Management
- [ ] **Quantity Controls:** Can users easily adjust quantities using +/- buttons?
- [ ] **Remove Item Flow:** Is removing an item simple? Is there an "Undo" or
      "Save for Later" option?
- [ ] **Navigation:** Is there a clear "Continue Shopping" or "Back" path that
      doesn't feel like "escaping"?
- [ ] **Cart Type:** Is the cart type (Drawer vs. Full Page) appropriate for the
      catalog size?

## 4. Trust & Reassurance
- [ ] **Security Icons:** Is there a lock icon or "Secure Checkout" text near
       the button?
- [ ] **Accepted Payments:** Are logos of accepted payment methods visible?
- [ ] **Guarantees:** Are return policies or satisfaction guarantees mentioned
      briefly?
- [ ] **Privacy Promise:** If an email is requested, is there a short privacy
      assurance?

## 5. Friction Reduction
- [ ] **Coupon Prominence:** Is the promo code box de-emphasized to prevent
      users from leaving to find codes?
- [ ] **Upsell Balance:** Do "Recommended for you" items take up less than 25%
      of the cart's visual space?
- [ ] **Loading Speed:** Does the cart load or update in under 500ms?

---

## Audit Scoring
- **18-21 Checkmarks:** **High Conversion.** Your cart is a frictionless gateway.
- **12-17 Checkmarks:** **Optimizable.** You have transparency or UX gaps that are
  likely leaking revenue.
- **Below 12 Checkmarks:** **Critical Friction.** Your cart is a major bottleneck
  in your growth funnel.
