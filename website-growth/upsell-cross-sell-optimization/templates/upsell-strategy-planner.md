## Part 2: Funnel Placement Audit

Where are you currently asking for the "Extra"?

- [ ] **PDP (Product Detail Page):** Are comparison tables or "Frequently
      Bought Together" widgets present?
- [ ] **Add-to-Cart (Interstitial):** Is there a low-friction prompt for
      essentials?
- [ ] **Cart Page:** Are "Progress Bars" used to encourage hitting a free
      shipping/discount threshold?
- [ ] **Checkout (First Step):** Are "Order Bumps" (checkboxes for small add-ons)
      visible?
- [ ] **Post-Purchase (Thank You):** Is there a one-click offer for a
      complementary item or subscription?

---

## Part 3: Offer Crafting Checklist

For every proposed upsell/cross-sell, ensure it meets these criteria:

1.  **[ ] The 25% Rule:** Is the add-on price < 25% of the primary item's price?
2.  **[ ] The Relevance Test:** If a user bought [Primary], would they
    *actually* need [Add-on] right now?
3.  **[ ] The Friction Check:** Can the user decline the offer in a single
    click?
4.  **[ ] The Value Hook:** Is there a specific benefit to buying *now* (e.g.,
    bundle discount, free shipping, time-limited offer)?
5.  **[ ] The Proof Point:** Is there a trust signal (e.g., "Top-rated add-on")?
