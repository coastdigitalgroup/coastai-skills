# Case Study Optimization & Audit Checklist

Use this template to audit your existing customer case studies or to structure new ones for maximum conversion impact.

## 1. The "Hero Metric" & Headline
- [ ] **Data-Driven Headline:** Does the headline include a specific result? (e.g., "How [Company] achieved [X]% [Benefit]")
- [ ] **The "10-Second" Rule:** If a user only reads the headline and the first callout, do they understand the value?
- [ ] **Relatability:** Is the featured company/persona clearly identified so the target reader can say "That's like me"?

## 2. Problem (The Friction)
- [ ] **Quantified Pain:** Is the "Before" state described with specific costs, time lost, or missed opportunities?
- [ ] **Emotional Resonance:** Does it describe the frustration or business pressure the customer felt?
- [ ] **The "Inaction" Cost:** Does it explain what would have happened if they didn't find a solution?

## 3. Solution (The Implementation)
- [ ] **Repeatable Process:** Is it clear *which* specific features or services were used?
- [ ] **Overcoming Objections:** Does it mention how common implementation fears (cost, time, complexity) were handled?
- [ ] **The "Aha!" Moment:** Is there a specific point in the story where the solution started working?

## 4. Result (The Outcome)
- [ ] **The "Hero" Number:** Is there at least one hard metric (%, $, or Time) highlighted?
- [ ] **Secondary Benefits:** Are there "soft" benefits mentioned (e.g., team morale, brand reputation)?
- [ ] **The "Anchor" Quote:** Is there a direct, attributable quote from a high-level stakeholder?

## 5. Visual Hierarchy & Scannability
- [ ] **Results Sidebar/Bar:** Is there an "At-a-Glance" section for skimmers?
- [ ] **Scannable Subheads:** Do subheadlines describe the *result* rather than the *phase*? (e.g., "Saving 20 hours a week" vs. "The Result")
- [ ] **Identity Proof:** Are there real logos and headshots (not stock photos)?
- [ ] **Call to Action (CTA):** Is there a clear, relevant next step at the bottom of the page?

---

## Audit Scoring Rubric

| Score | Status | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **0-5** | ❌ **Passive Narrative** | Mostly text, no data, vague outcomes. Acts as a "blog post" rather than a sales tool. |
| **6-10** | ⚠️ **Emerging Proof** | Some data included, but hard to find. Lacks scannability and a clear CTA. |
| **11-15** | ✅ **Conversion Asset** | Strong PSR structure, clear hero metrics, scannable layout, and relevant CTA. |
