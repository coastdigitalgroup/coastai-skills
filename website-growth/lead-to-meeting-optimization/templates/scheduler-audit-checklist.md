# Lead-to-Meeting Friction Audit Checklist

Use this checklist to identify bottlenecks in your transition from lead capture to confirmed meeting.

## 1. The Post-Submission Experience
- [ ] **Instant Feedback:** Does the "Submit" button provide immediate visual feedback (e.g., loading spinner) to prevent double-clicks?
- [ ] **Momentum Continuity:** Is the user redirected to a page that *specifically* addresses the meeting, or just a generic "Thank You" page?
- [ ] **The 10-Second Test:** Can a qualified user reach a calendar interface within 10 seconds of hitting "Submit"?
- [ ] **Elimination of "The Wait":** Are you avoiding phrases like "We'll be in touch soon" for high-priority leads?

## 2. Instant Scheduling Logic
- [ ] **Qualification Filters:** Are you using form data (Company size, budget, etc.) to decide who sees a calendar?
- [ ] **Automatic Routing:** Are leads being routed to the correct owner (SDR vs. AE) automatically based on your sales rules?
- [ ] **Data Persistence:** Does the scheduling tool automatically inherit the Name and Email from the lead form (no re-typing)?
- [ ] **Timezone Awareness:** Does the calendar automatically detect and display slots in the prospect's local timezone?

## 3. The Booking Interface UX
- [ ] **Mobile Responsiveness:** Is the calendar widget easy to use on a smartphone (no tiny tap targets)?
- [ ] **Minimalist Layout:** Is the calendar the primary focus of the page, or is it surrounded by distracting navigation links?
- [ ] **Immediate Availability:** Are there at least 3 available slots within the next 48-72 hours? (Nothing kills momentum like a 2-week wait).
- [ ] **Clear Value Prop:** Does the header above the calendar explain exactly what the user will gain from the meeting?

## 4. Confirmation & Risk Reversal
- [ ] **Calendar Invite:** Is a calendar invite sent *instantly* upon booking?
- [ ] **Agenda Clarity:** Does the confirmation screen or email include a 3-point agenda for the call?
- [ ] **Easy Rescheduling:** Is there a clear "Reschedule" or "Cancel" link in the confirmation email to prevent ghosting?
- [ ] **Secondary Assets:** Does the "Booking Confirmed" page provide a video or case study for the user to watch while they wait for the call?

## 5. Technical Health
- [ ] **CRM Sync:** Is the meeting activity automatically logged in the CRM against the lead record?
- [ ] **Tracking Pixels:** Are you tracking "Meeting Booked" as a conversion event in your analytics (separate from "Lead Captured")?
- [ ] **Error Handling:** If the calendar fails to load, is there a fallback "Call Us" or "Email Us" option visible?
