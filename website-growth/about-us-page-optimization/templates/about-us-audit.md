# About Us Page: Growth & Trust Audit Checklist

Use this template to evaluate if an "About Us" page is working as a conversion
asset or acting as a dead-end.

## 1. The Narrative & Copy
- [ ] **Customer-Centric Hook:** Does the first headline mention a customer
  benefit or problem solved?
- [ ] **The "We" vs. "You" Balance:** Does the copy focus more on the
  customer's success than the company's internal history?
- [ ] **Jargon Check:** Is the mission stated in plain English that a new
  customer can understand?
- [ ] **The Origin Story:** Is there a clear "Why" that explains the passion
  behind the product?

## 2. Human Connection & Visuals
- [ ] **Authentic Photos:** Are there real photos of founders or team members
  (No Stock People)?
- [ ] **Leadership Transparency:** Are names and professional backgrounds
  provided for key leaders?
- [ ] **Personality:** Does the tone of the page match the brand's personality
  (e.g., professional, quirky, innovative)?
- [ ] **Visual Hierarchy:** Is the page easy to skim with bold headers and
  pull-quotes?

## 3. Trust & Authority Signals
- [ ] **Social Proof:** Are there mentions of major clients, partners, or
  investors?
- [ ] **Industry Validation:** Are awards, certifications, or professional
  affiliations visible?
- [ ] **Longevity/Stability:** Is there a timeline or mention of years in
  business to reduce "fly-by-night" anxiety?
- [ ] **Media Mentions:** If applicable, are there "As seen in" logos or press
  links?

## 4. Conversion & Flow
- [ ] **Internal Linking:** Does the narrative link to key services,
  products, or content (e.g., "Our Process," "Case Studies")?
- [ ] **The Bottom CTA:** Is there a clear "Next Step" button at the very
  bottom of the page?
- [ ] **Soft Conversion:** Is there a secondary action (e.g., Newsletter,
  Social Follow) for users not yet ready to buy?

## 5. Mobile & Performance
- [ ] **Readability:** Is the text easy to read on a mobile device (font size
  and contrast)?
- [ ] **Image Optimization:** Do the team photos load quickly without
  causing layout shifts?

---

### Audit Scoring:
*   **0-5 Checkmarks:** **Critical.** The page is likely a major drop-off point.
*   **6-10 Checkmarks:** **Passive.** The page exists but isn't actively
    building trust or driving growth.
*   **11-15 Checkmarks:** **Optimized.** The page is a strategic asset in the
    buyer's journey.
