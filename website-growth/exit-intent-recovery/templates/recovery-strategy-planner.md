interventions that recover value from abandoning users.

## Part 3: Intervention Design Checklist

For every proposed exit-intent popup, ensure it meets these "Growth Heuristics":

1. **[ ] The 3-Second Rule:** Can the user understand the offer and the value in
   under 3 seconds?
2. **[ ] The Friction Check:** Does it require more than two fields? (Aim for
   one: Email).
3. **[ ] The Mobile Exit:** Is the "Close" button at least 44x44px and easy to
   tap?
4. **[ ] The Logic Filter:** Is it set to _not_ show to users who have already
   converted?
5. **[ ] The Benefit Hook:** Is the headline a "Statement of Value" rather than
   just "Wait!"?
