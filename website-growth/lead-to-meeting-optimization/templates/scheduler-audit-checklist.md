# Lead-to-Meeting Audit Checklist

Use this template to evaluate the efficiency of your current lead-to-meeting transition.

## 1. Speed-to-Lead Assessment
- [ ] **Initial Response Time:** How long does it take for a lead to get a booking link after submitting a form?
    - *Target:* < 1 minute (Instant).
- [ ] **First Human Contact:** For leads not instantly booked, how long until an SDR reaches out?
    - *Target:* < 5 minutes.
- [ ] **Weekend/After-Hours:** Is there an automated path for leads that come in when the team is offline?

## 2. Friction & User Experience
- [ ] **Field Redundancy:** Does the user have to enter their Name/Email twice (once on the form, once on the scheduler)?
- [ ] **Step Count:** How many pages/clicks are required from "Submit" to "Confirmed"?
    - *Target:* < 3 clicks.
- [ ] **Mobile Optimization:** Is the calendar widget easy to use on a mobile device without excessive zooming?
- [ ] **CTA Clarity:** Is the next step clearly stated on the Thank You page? (e.g., "Choose a time below" vs. "We'll be in touch").

## 3. Routing & Qualification Logic
- [ ] **High-Fit Filtering:** Are high-value leads (based on company size, budget, etc.) given an immediate path to book?
- [ ] **Round-Robin Efficiency:** Is the routing logic balanced across the sales team to ensure availability?
- [ ] **Disqualified Path:** Is there a polite "fallback" for leads that don't meet qualification criteria (e.g., redirect to a self-serve demo)?

## 4. Confirmation & Retention (Reducing No-Shows)
- [ ] **Instant Calendar Invite:** Is a `.ics` or calendar invitation sent within 30 seconds of booking?
- [ ] **Reminder Sequence:** Are there automated reminders at the 24-hour and 1-hour marks?
- [ ] **Agenda Setting:** Does the confirmation email clearly state what will be covered and who will be on the call?
- [ ] **Easy Rescheduling:** Is there a "Reschedule" link in every email to prevent ghosting?

## 5. Technical Health
- [ ] **CRM Sync:** Are all booking events correctly logged against the lead record in the CRM?
- [ ] **UTM Attribution:** Is the original traffic source (Ads, Organic, etc.) preserved through the booking process?
- [ ] **Buffer Times:** Are there adequate buffers between meetings to prevent sales burnout and back-to-back delays?
