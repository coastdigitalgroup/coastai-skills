# Example: Optimizing a B2B SaaS Case Study

This example demonstrates how to transform a standard, chronological case study into a high-conversion sales asset using the PSR framework.

## Before Optimization: The "Chronological Report"

*   **Title:** How AcmeCorp Implemented Our CRM
*   **Structure:**
    1.  Introduction to AcmeCorp.
    2.  Our project kickoff and implementation timeline.
    3.  A list of CRM features they used.
    4.  A closing paragraph saying they are "very happy" with the results.
*   **Measurable Outcome:** 0.5% CTA Click-Through Rate (CTR); 70% bounce rate within the first 30 seconds.

**The Problem:** The value is buried at the bottom. The headline is about the *process* (implementation), not the *benefit*. There are no scannable data points.

---

## After Optimization: The "High-Conversion Asset"

*   **Title:** How AcmeCorp Reduced Lead Response Time by 85% and Boosted Sales by 22%
*   **Structure:**
    1.  **Result Hook:** Immediate callout of the lead response and sales metrics with a high-quality photo of the AcmeCorp VP of Sales.
    2.  **The Problem (The PSR Phase):** AcmeCorp was losing 1 in 4 leads because their manual spreadsheets took 24+ hours to route to sales reps.
    3.  **The Solution (The PSR Phase):** Automated Lead Routing and Real-Time Slack Notifications. A short "How it Works" visual showing the data flow.
    4.  **The Result (The PSR Phase):** Lead response dropped from 24 hours to 8 minutes. Sales increased by 22% in the first quarter.
    5.  **Contextual CTA:** "See how our Automated Routing can work for your team [Book a Demo]."
*   **Measurable Outcome:** 4.2% CTA Click-Through Rate (CTR); 45% increase in scroll depth; 12% decrease in bounce rate.

---

## Key Changes Made

| Feature | Before | After |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Headline** | Process-oriented ("How we did it") | Outcome-oriented ("What they gained") |
| **Data Visibility** | Buried in paragraphs | Highlighted in a "Results at a Glance" sidebar |
| **Narrative** | Chronological ("First we did X...") | Persuasive ("They had problem Y, we did Z, they got result A") |
| **CTAs** | Single link at the very bottom | Contextual links after major result milestones |
| **Social Proof** | Generic "We're happy" quote | Specific quote linking the tool to the 22% sales lift |
