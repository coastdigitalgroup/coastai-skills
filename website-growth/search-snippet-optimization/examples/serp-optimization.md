# Example: Search Snippet Optimization for E-commerce

This example demonstrates how optimizing a search snippet for a high-volume category page can drive a significant lift in organic traffic by improving the Click-Through Rate (CTR).

## Scenario: Premium Coffee Roaster

**The Problem:**
A specialty coffee roaster ranks #3 for the high-intent keyword "organic espresso beans." Despite the high ranking, the page is receiving a lower-than-expected CTR (5.2% vs. industry average of ~9% for position #3).

**Current Snippet (The "Before"):**
- **Title:** Espresso Beans - Organic - [Brand Name]
- **Description:** We have the best organic espresso beans. Our beans are roasted fresh and delivered to your door. Buy now.
- **Visual:** Standard text-only snippet. No stars, price, or availability.

---

## The Optimization (The "After")

Applying the **Search Snippet Optimization** skill:

### 1. Audit & Analysis
- **GSC Data:** 100,000 monthly impressions, 5,200 clicks (5.2% CTR).
- **Competitor Scan:** The #1 and #2 spots are using Review stars and showing prices.
- **Key Lever:** Implementing `Product` schema and refining copy to highlight "Freshness" (a key customer driver).

### 2. Optimized Meta Specification
- **Title:** Organic Espresso Beans | Fresh Roasted & Small Batch | [Brand Name]
  - *Why:* Leads with the keyword, highlights the "Small Batch" value prop, and uses a separator.
- **Description:** Experience the rich, bold flavor of our 100% Organic Espresso Beans. Roasted daily in Seattle. Free shipping on $50+. Shop our award-winning blends today!
  - *Why:* Mentions freshness ("Roasted daily"), adds a location trust signal, includes a specific offer ("Free shipping"), and a clear CTA.

### 3. Rich Snippet Implementation
- **Schema.org:** Added `Product` markup with `AggregateRating` and `Offers`.
- **Resulting Snippet:**
  - **Stars:** ★★★★★ (4.9/5 based on 850 reviews)
  - **Price:** From $18.00
  - **Availability:** In Stock

---

## Measurable Outcome

After 30 days of the new snippet being indexed:

| Metric | Before | After | Change |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **CTR** | 5.2% | 8.8% | **+69.2%** |
| **Monthly Clicks** | 5,200 | 8,800 | **+3,600** |
| **Avg. Position** | 3.2 | 3.1 | (Stable) |
| **Revenue (Est.)** | $12,480 | $21,120 | **+$8,640** |

**Conclusion:**
By making the search result more visually prominent with stars and more persuasive with benefit-driven copy, the brand captured an additional 3,600 visitors per month without needing to spend more on ads or fighting for the #1 ranking spot.
