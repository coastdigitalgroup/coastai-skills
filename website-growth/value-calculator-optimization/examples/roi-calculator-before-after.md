# Example: ROI Calculator Optimization

## Scenario: Enterprise Fleet Management Software

**The Company:** "FleetFlow," a SaaS provider for logistics companies.
**The Problem:** Their "Savings Estimator" had a 15% completion rate. Users
dropped off at the third question because they didn't know their "Average
Maintenance Cost per Vehicle per Month."

### Before: The "Interrogation" Flow

1.  **Lead Wall:** User must enter Name and Email to start.
2.  **12 Manual Inputs:** All fields were empty text boxes.
3.  **High-Friction Questions:** "Enter your exact annual fuel expenditure."
4.  **Static Result:** After 12 steps, a message said: "We've emailed you your
    results."

**Outcome:** High bounce rate, low lead volume, and "low-intent" leads who
entered fake data just to see what happened.

---

### After: The Optimized Value Experience

1.  **Immediate Start:** No lead wall. The first question is "How many vehicles
    are in your fleet?" (Single slider).
2.  **Sensible Defaults:** As soon as "50 vehicles" is selected, the calculator
    pre-fills maintenance and fuel costs based on national industry averages
    (e.g., "$450/month"). A small subtext says: *"Based on industry averages.
    Click to customize."*
3.  **The "Momentum" Hook:** A large "Estimated Annual Savings" counter at the
    bottom of the screen updates in real-time ($0 -> $45,000) as the user
    adjusts the fleet size slider.
4.  **The Labor Illusion:** Before showing the final breakdown, a 2-second
    animation says: *"Analyzing your fleet data against logistics benchmarks..."*
5.  **The Value-Exchange Lead:** The results page shows a high-level chart of
    savings. To get the "Full Fleet Efficiency Audit & PDF Report," the user
    enters their email.

---

### Results (Measurable Outcome)

-   **Completion Rate:** Increased from 15% to **68%**.
-   **Lead Volume:** Increased by **310%** monthly.
-   **Sales Impact:** Sales team reported that prospects were using the
    "Audit PDF" in their internal budget meetings to justify the purchase.
-   **CPL (Cost Per Lead):** Decreased by 45% due to higher organic conversion.
