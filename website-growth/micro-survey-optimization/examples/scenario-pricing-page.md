# Micro-Survey Optimization: Before & After

This example demonstrates how a SaaS company used a contextual micro-survey to
identify a hidden conversion blocker on their pricing page.

## Scenario: The "High-Exit" Pricing Page

**The Product:** A project management tool for creative agencies.
**The Problem:** High exit rate (72%) on the pricing page. Users spend an average
of 50 seconds on the page—long enough to read—but leave without starting a
trial.

---

## Before: The "Passive Feedback" Tab

### The Experience
- **Trigger:** A small "Feedback" button stuck to the side of the screen.
- **Question:** "How can we improve this page? [Open Text Box]"
- **Outcome:** The team received almost no responses. The ones they did get
  were generic ("looks good") or unrelated to the product.

### Results
- **Response Rate:** 0.1%
- **Actionable Insights:** 0
- **Pricing Page CVR:** 2.4%

---

## After: The "Intent-Driven" Micro-Survey

### The Optimization
1.  **Trigger:** Exit-intent (desktop) and 45-second time-on-page (mobile).
2.  **The Hook:** "What's the #1 thing stopping you from starting a trial
    today?"
3.  **The Choices (Micro-Hook):**
    - [ ] "I'm not sure if it integrates with [Tool X]."
    - [ ] "It's more expensive than I expected."
    - [ ] "I need to talk to my team first."
    - [ ] "Other (please specify)"
4.  **The Follow-up:** If "Integrations" was selected, a text box appeared
    asking "Which tool do you need to integrate with?"

### Results
- **Response Rate:** 4.2% (A 42x increase)
- **The "Aha!" Discovery:** 55% of respondents selected "I need to talk to my
  team first."
- **Secondary Discovery:** Many "Other" responses mentioned they didn't see a
  way to export data if they wanted to leave later.

---

## The Transformation (A/B Test)

Armed with the "Why" from the micro-survey, the team didn't lower the price.
Instead, they added two specific elements to the pricing page:
1.  **"Pitch Your Boss" PDF:** A one-page guide linked next to the pricing cards
    that users could share with their managers.
2.  **"No Lock-In" Guarantee:** A small badge explaining the easy data export
    process.

### The Outcome
- **Pricing Page CVR:** Increased from 2.4% to 3.1% (**+29% lift**).
- **Revenue Impact:** $12,000 increase in Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
  within the first month.

---

## Key Takeaway
By shifting from a passive, vague question to a proactive, choice-driven
interaction, the team moved from "guessing" to "knowing," allowing them to
solve the *actual* psychological barrier preventing conversion.
