# Discount Strategy Examples: Before & After

## Scenario 1: Reducing "Coupon Hunt" Abandonment

### Before (The Friction Point)
A high-fashion e-commerce site has a prominent "Promo Code" input box at the very first step of the checkout.
*   **Behavior:** Analytics show that 25% of users who reach this step leave the site immediately after clicking the box.
*   **Result:** High abandonment. Users go to "coupon sites," get distracted by competitors, or find expired codes and return frustrated.
*   **Metric:** 68% Checkout Abandonment Rate.

### After (The Optimization)
1.  **Visual Subordination:** The input box is replaced with a small, gray text link that says "Have a promo code?".
2.  **Internal Fulfillment:** Clicking the link opens the field AND displays a "Today's Offers" section showing a valid 10% welcome code for new subscribers.
3.  **Auto-Apply:** All marketing emails now use URLs that auto-apply the discount, showing a "Success! Your 15% discount has been applied" banner in the cart.
*   **Result:** Reduced exit intent. Users stay in the funnel because the value is provided natively.
*   **Measurable Outcome:** Checkout Abandonment Rate dropped to 54%. Overall Conversion Rate increased by 12%.

## Scenario 2: Tiered Discounting for AOV Growth

### Before (The Generic Offer)
A supplement brand offers a flat "15% Off Site-wide" during a summer sale.
*   **Metric:** Average Order Value (AOV) is $45.
*   **Problem:** Most users buy one bottle ($30-40) and take the discount, leaving the brand with low margins after shipping.

### After (The Tiered Offer)
The brand switches to a tiered "Spend More, Save More" model:
*   **Tier 1:** Spend $60, Save $10 (Encourages a 2nd bottle)
*   **Tier 2:** Spend $100, Save $25 (Encourages a 3rd/4th bottle)
*   **UI Implementation:** A dynamic progress bar in the cart says: "Add one more bottle to save $10 instantly!"
*   **Result:** Users perceive higher value by "unlocking" the next tier.
*   **Measurable Outcome:** AOV increased from $45 to $72. Total Revenue increased by 28% despite higher total discount volume.
