# Micro-Survey Design & Deployment Checklist

Use this checklist to ensure your micro-surveys are high-converting, low-friction, and produce actionable insights.

## Phase 1: Strategic Planning
- [ ] **Define the "Missing Link":** What specific qualitative information is missing from your quantitative data?
- [ ] **Identify the High-Impact Page:** Where is the most critical drop-off occurring?
- [ ] **Targeting Logic:** Is this for everyone, or only specific segments (e.g., first-time visitors, mobile users, users with items in cart)?

## Phase 2: Question Design
- [ ] **The "Golden Rule":** Is the first question a "single-tap" interaction (Multiple Choice, Stars, or Emoji)?
- [ ] **Action-Oriented Phrasing:** Does the question focus on barriers to action rather than general feelings?
- [ ] **"Other" Inclusion:** Is there an open-ended option to capture "Black Swan" insights you didn't anticipate?
- [ ] **Bias Check:** Does the question avoid leading the user? (e.g., avoid "How easy was this?" and use "How would you rate the ease of this process?")
- [ ] **Length Check:** Is the entire survey 1–3 questions maximum?

## Phase 3: Trigger & Technical Setup
- [ ] **Context-Aware Triggering:**
    - [ ] **Exit-Intent:** For understanding "Why people leave."
    - [ ] **Scroll Depth (>50%):** For gauging engagement with long-form content.
    - [ ] **Time on Page (>45s):** For identifying "Stuck" users.
- [ ] **Persistence Rules:** Is the survey set to stop appearing once a user has seen or dismissed it? (Avoid "Survey Spam").
- [ ] **Mobile Optimization:** Are the buttons large enough to tap easily? Does it cover the main CTA? (It shouldn't!)

## Phase 4: Analysis & Action
- [ ] **The "Bucketing" Plan:** Who is responsible for categorizing the qualitative responses every week?
- [ ] **A/B Test Pipeline:** How will these insights be turned into A/B test hypotheses?
- [ ] **Closing the Loop:** Do we have a "Thank You" screen that provides value or a next step?

## Recommended Question Bank

| Goal | Recommended Question |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Exit Recovery** | "What's the one thing that nearly stopped you from [Action] today?" |
| **Intent Mapping** | "What was your primary goal for visiting our site today?" |
| **Clarity Check** | "Is there any information missing from this page that would make your decision easier?" |
| **Search Fail** | "Were you able to find what you were looking for?" |
| **Post-Purchase** | "What's the one thing that almost made you *not* buy from us?" |
