# Inclusive Growth Audit Checklist

Use this template to audit your conversion funnel for inclusivity gaps that
are leaking potential market share.

## 1. Perception (Can they see/hear the value?)

- [ ] **Contrast Check:** Do all primary CTAs and headlines have a contrast
  ratio of at least 4.5:1 against the background?
- [ ] **Color Blindness Test:** If you remove all color (grayscale), are the
  primary actions and status indicators (e.g., "In Stock") still clear?
- [ ] **Alt-Text Audit:** Do all conversion-critical images (product shots,
  value-prop icons) have descriptive alt-text that conveys their meaning?
- [ ] **Video Accessibility:** Do all auto-playing or conversion-focused
  videos have captions or a transcript?

## 2. Operation (Can they interact with the goal?)

- [ ] **Keyboard Path:** Can a user navigate from the homepage to "Order
  Success" using only the `Tab`, `Shift+Tab`, `Space`, and `Enter` keys?
- [ ] **Focus Visibility:** Is there a clear, high-contrast visual indicator
  around the element that currently has keyboard focus?
- [ ] **Tap Target Size:** Are all buttons, links, and form fields at least
  44x44 pixels in size?
- [ ] **Form Time Limits:** If your checkout has a "session timeout," is
  there a warning and a way to extend it before the user loses their work?

## 3. Understanding (Can they comprehend the path?)

- [ ] **Error Clarity:** Do error messages explain *exactly* what is wrong
  and *how* to fix it (e.g., "Enter a 5-digit zip code" vs. "Invalid Input")?
- [ ] **Reading Level:** Is the core instructional text written at an 8th-grade
  level or lower? (Use Hemingway or similar tools).
- [ ] **Consistency:** Are the icons and labels for "Add to Cart," "Search,"
  and "Checkout" consistent across the entire site?
- [ ] **Cognitive Ease:** Are complex forms broken into smaller, logical
  steps with a clear progress indicator?

## 4. Robustness (Does it work everywhere?)

- [ ] **Text Resizing:** Does the layout remain functional and readable when
  the browser font size is increased to 200%?
- [ ] **Orientation:** Is the site fully functional in both Portrait and
  Landscape modes on mobile?
- [ ] **High Contrast Mode:** Does the site remain usable when Windows High
  Contrast Mode or Mac Invert Colors is enabled?
