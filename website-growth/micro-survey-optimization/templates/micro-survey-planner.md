# Micro-Survey Planning Template

Use this template to plan and audit on-site micro-surveys to ensure they are high-impact and low-friction.

## 1. Goal Identification
*Define exactly what you are trying to learn.*

- **The "Why":** What specific user behavior are we trying to understand? (e.g., "Why do users bounce from the checkout page?")
- **The Hypothesis:** What do we *think* is the problem? (e.g., "We think users are surprised by shipping costs.")
- **Actionability:** If we find out [X] is the problem, what is the specific change we will make to the site?

## 2. Targeting & Triggers
*Define who sees the survey and when.*

- **Target Page(s):** (URL or Page Type)
- **Audience Segment:**
  - [ ] New visitors
  - [ ] Returning visitors
  - [ ] Traffic from specific source (e.g., Paid Ads)
  - [ ] Users on [Mobile/Desktop]
- **Trigger Rule:**
  - [ ] Exit-intent (mouse-out)
  - [ ] Time on page (> X seconds)
  - [ ] Scroll depth (> X%)
  - [ ] After clicking [Specific Element]

## 3. Question Design
*Draft the interaction flow.*

- **Question 1 (The Hook):** (Must be low-friction, ideally multiple choice or Yes/No)
- **Options:** (Limit to 3-5 clear choices)
- **Question 2 (The Depth - Optional):** (Open-ended "Tell us more...")
- **Thank You Message:** (Short and benefit-driven, e.g., "Thanks! We'll use your feedback to improve the site.")

## 4. Optimization Checklist
*Review the survey against these standards.*

- [ ] **Low Friction:** Does it start with a single click?
- [ ] **Contextual:** Is the question highly relevant to the page it's on?
- [ ] **Unbiased:** Is the question neutral (not "leading")?
- [ ] **Mobile-Safe:** Does it avoid blocking the primary CTA on mobile?
- [ ] **Frequency-Capped:** Is it set to "Show once per visitor"?
- [ ] **Exit-Aware:** Is the "X" (close) button easy to find?

## 5. Results & Analysis
*Framework for acting on the data.*

| Response Theme | Frequency (%) | Sample Quote | Priority (H/M/L) | Planned Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| e.g., Price | 40% | "Too expensive compared to X" | High | Test lower entry tier |
| e.g., Clarity | 20% | "Don't see where to log in" | Medium | Improve nav visibility |
