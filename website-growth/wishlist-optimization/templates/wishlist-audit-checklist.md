# Wishlist Optimization Audit Checklist

Use this checklist to evaluate the effectiveness of a website's wishlist or "save
for later" functionality.

## 1. Discovery & Visibility
- [ ] Is there a "Save" or "Wishlist" icon visible on the **Product Listing Page
  (PLP)** grid?
- [ ] Is the "Save" button prominently placed near the "Add to Cart" button on
  the **Product Detail Page (PDP)**?
- [ ] Is there a "Wishlist" entry point in the **Global Header** (ideally near
  the Cart)?
- [ ] Does the wishlist icon have a **numerical badge** (e.g., "Heart (3)") to
  remind users of their saved items?

## 2. Friction & Accessibility
- [ ] **The "Guest" Test:** Can a new user save an item without being forced to
  create an account or log in?
- [ ] Does the "Save" action provide **immediate visual feedback** (e.g., an
  animation or a "Saved!" toast message)?
- [ ] Is the touch target for the "Save" icon on mobile at least **44x44px**?
- [ ] Does the wishlist **persist across sessions** for anonymous users (using
  cookies or local storage)?

## 3. The Wishlist Page Experience
- [ ] Does every item in the wishlist have a clear **"Add to Cart"** button?
- [ ] Does the list show **live inventory status** (e.g., "In Stock," "Low
  Stock," "Sold Out")?
- [ ] Does the list show **current pricing** and highlight any recent price
  drops?
- [ ] Are items displayed with **high-quality thumbnails** and titles for quick
  recognition?
- [ ] Is there a **"Move to Cart"** vs. **"Copy to Cart"** distinction to allow
  users to manage their list?

## 4. The Cart "Safety Net"
- [ ] Is there a **"Move to Wishlist"** or "Save for Later" option for items
  currently in the cart?
- [ ] When an item is moved to the wishlist from the cart, is it confirmed
  visually so the user knows they haven't "lost" it?
- [ ] Are wishlist items **suggested in the cart** as "You might also like
  these items from your wishlist"?

## 5. Re-engagement & Triggers
- [ ] Are there automated **Price Drop** notifications for wishlisted items?
- [ ] Are there automated **Back in Stock** notifications?
- [ ] Are there **"Low Stock"** warnings (e.g., "Only 2 left of your saved
  items")?
- [ ] Is there a periodic **"Wishlist Digest"** email for users who haven't
  visited in 14+ days?

## 6. Social & Sharing (If Applicable)
- [ ] Can the user **share their wishlist** via a public or private link?
- [ ] Are there **social sharing buttons** (Email, WhatsApp, Pinterest) directly
  on the wishlist page?
- [ ] Can multiple people **collaborate** on a single list (for events or group
  buys)?
