## AFTER: The Optimized PSR Narrative

**Title: How Global Fab Recovered 20% of "Lost" Leads and Saved 15 Hours a Week**

**The Outcome Summary:**
- **20% Increase** in lead-to-opportunity conversion.
- **15 Hours/Week** saved in manual data entry.
- **Full Visibility** into sales pipeline for the executive team.

**The Problem: The "Spreadsheet Leak"**
Global Fab was losing nearly $15,000 in potential revenue every month because
leads were falling through the cracks of their manual Excel tracking system.
Sales reps were spending **30% of their day** on data entry instead of selling,
and follow-up times averaged over 48 hours.

**The Solution: Automated Lead Capture & Follow-up**
Using PipelinePro’s automated lead routing, Global Fab eliminated manual entry
entirely. We implemented:
1. **Instant Web-to-Lead Sync:** Leads from the website now hit a rep's mobile
   device in under 60 seconds.
2. **Automated Follow-up Sequences:** Ensuring no lead goes cold during the
   first 7 days.
3. **Custom "Manufacturing Flow" Dashboard:** Giving managers real-time data
   on production-ready leads.

**The Result: From Chaos to Conversion**
Within 90 days of implementation, Global Fab didn't just "stay organized"—they
scaled.
- **Speed to Lead:** Dropped from 48 hours to **5 minutes**.
- **ROI:** The system paid for itself in the first 30 days by recovering two
  high-value "lost" accounts.

> "Before PipelinePro, we were guessing. Now we’re growing. We recovered
> $30k in sales in the first quarter simply by not letting leads go cold."
> — **Sarah Jenkins**, Operations Manager at Global Fab
