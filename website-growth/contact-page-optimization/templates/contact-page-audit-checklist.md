# Contact Page Audit Checklist

Use this checklist to evaluate if a contact page is optimized for conversion, routing, and trust.

## 1. Intent & Routing
- [ ] **Is there a "Routing Hub"?** Does the page ask the user what they need (Sales, Support, Billing) before showing a form?
- [ ] **Are high-intent actions prioritized?** Is the "Sales/Quote" path more visually prominent than "Support"?
- [ ] **Are forms customized?** Does the Sales form ask for qualification data (Budget, Size) while the Support form asks for resolution data (Order #)?
- [ ] **Is there a "Self-Service" escape hatch?** Are support seekers guided toward a Knowledge Base or FAQ before contacting a human?

## 2. Trust & Authority
- [ ] **Human Evidence:** Are there photos of real people, the office, or the team?
- [ ] **Physical Evidence:** Is there a real physical address and map listed?
- [ ] **Social Proof:** Are there trust badges, testimonials, or "Trusted by..." logos on the page?
- [ ] **Brand Consistency:** Does the tone of the contact page match the rest of the site?

## 3. Transparency & Expectations
- [ ] **Response Guarantee:** Is there a clear statement of *when* the user should expect a reply (e.g., "Within 4 business hours")?
- [ ] **Primary Action:** Is it clear what the *best* way to get a fast response is (e.g., "Live chat is fastest")?
- [ ] **Operating Hours:** Are business hours clearly listed in the user's local time (or with a GMT offset)?
- [ ] **Success State:** Does the thank-you message tell the user exactly what happens next and who will reach out?

## 4. Friction & Usability
- [ ] **Field Count:** Are there unnecessary fields in the general inquiry form (e.g., "Phone" for a simple question)?
- [ ] **Mobile Optimization:** Is the form easy to fill out on a thumb-driven device? (Tap targets > 44px).
- [ ] **Keyboard Support:** Do numeric fields (Phone, Zip) trigger the correct mobile keyboard?
- [ ] **Spam Protection:** Is the form protected by invisible reCAPTCHA or a honeypot (rather than a frustrating "Puzzle" captcha)?

- [ ] **Current Initial Response Time (IRT):** ________h
- [ ] **Support Noise in Sales Inbox:** ________%
