# Customer Case Study Optimization Checklist

Use this checklist to audit existing case studies or to structure new ones for maximum conversion impact.

## 1. Headline & Immediate Value (The 5-Second Test)
- [ ] Does the headline include a specific, measurable result (e.g., "30% Lift," "$1M Saved")?
- [ ] Is there an "Executive Summary" or "Results at a Glance" box above the fold?
- [ ] Can a user identify the customer's industry and company size within 5 seconds?
- [ ] Is the primary "Win" visible without any scrolling?

## 2. Narrative Structure (The PSR Framework)
- [ ] **Problem:** Is the challenge described in terms of business impact (lost revenue, wasted time) rather than just technical frustration?
- [ ] **Solution:** Does the solution focus on *how the user's life improved* rather than just a list of product features?
- [ ] **Result:** Are the results data-backed (percentages, dollar amounts, hours saved)?
- [ ] **Emotion:** Is there a quote from the customer that addresses a common objection or fear (e.g., "Setup was surprisingly easy")?

## 3. Scannability & Design
- [ ] Are there clear H2/H3 headers that tell the story even if the body text is ignored?
- [ ] Are there visual breaks every 200-300 words (images, pull quotes, bullet points)?
- [ ] Is there at least one data visualization (chart, graph, or "Before/After" visual)?
- [ ] Are important numbers or "Hero Quotes" bolded or styled for high contrast?
- [ ] Is the text width optimized for readability (ideally 600px - 800px)?

## 4. Social Proof & Trust
- [ ] Is the customer's logo clearly displayed?
- [ ] Is there a photo of the person providing the quote (to prove authenticity)?
- [ ] Is the quote attributed to a specific person with a relevant title (e.g., "VP of Marketing")?
- [ ] Are there links to other "Related Case Studies" from the same industry?

## 5. Conversion Pathing (The Escape Hatch)
- [ ] Is there a primary CTA at the bottom of the page?
- [ ] Is the CTA copy contextually relevant (e.g., "Get the same results" vs. "Submit")?
- [ ] Is there a secondary CTA (e.g., "Watch Demo," "View Pricing") placed midway through the content?
- [ ] Does the page avoid "Dead Ends" (links to non-existent pages or broken forms)?
- [ ] On mobile, is the CTA button large enough (min 44x44px) and easy to find?

---

## Performance Scoring
- **0-10 Points:** Passive Narrative. High risk of bounce. Requires full restructuring.
- **11-18 Points:** Good Content, Poor Pathing. Valuable proof is being wasted.
- **19-25 Points:** Conversion Engine. Optimized for growth.
