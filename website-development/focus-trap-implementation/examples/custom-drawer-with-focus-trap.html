Keyboard Focus Trap Showcase

Demonstrating the difference between a broken side-drawer (where focus leaks back to the page) and a WCAG-compliant side-drawer utilizing our custom focus trap.

💡 Keyboard Navigation Instructions:

Use the Tab key to navigate through elements on this page. Open each drawer below to test focus behavior:

  • In the Broken Drawer: Open the drawer, then press Tab continuously. Notice focus wanders completely out of the drawer and highlights links/buttons in the main page behind it.
  • In the Focus-Trapped Drawer: Open the drawer. Notice focus is locked inside. Pressing Tab at the bottom loops focus back to the top. Pressing Escape or clicking the backdrop closes the drawer and restores focus perfectly to the opening button.

Broken Side Drawer NO FOCUS TRAP

This drawer visually slides open, but keyboard focus remains unaware. Focus will bleed into the main background page.

Accessible Side Drawer FOCUS TRAPPED

This drawer uses our FocusTrap utility. It traps focus, handles Shift+Tab, closes on Escape, and restores focus.
