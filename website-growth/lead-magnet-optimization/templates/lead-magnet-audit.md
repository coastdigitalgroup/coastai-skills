# Lead Magnet Audit & Optimization Checklist

Use this template to evaluate an existing lead magnet or to vet a new concept before development.

## 1. The "Value" Core
- [ ] **Specific Problem:** Does this solve a single, well-defined problem?
- [ ] **Specific Outcome:** Is the "win" or "result" clearly stated?
- [ ] **Immediate Utility:** Can the user get value within 5 minutes of opening it?
- [ ] **Unique Insight:** Does this provide something they can't easily find with a 10-second Google search?

## 2. Intent Alignment
- [ ] **Context Match:** Is the offer a logical next step for someone reading the current page?
- [ ] **Persona Match:** Is the tone and complexity appropriate for the target audience?
- [ ] **Awareness Level:** Does the offer match the user's stage (e.g., "What is X" for Unaware vs. "X Vendor Comparison" for Solution Aware)?

## 3. Friction & Presentation
- [ ] **The "Cost" Balance:** Are you asking for more data than the offer is worth? (e.g., Phone number for a 1-page PDF is usually too much).
- [ ] **Headline Clarity:** Does the headline focus on the *benefit* rather than the *format*?
- [ ] **Visual Mockup:** Is there a visual representation of the digital asset?
- [ ] **CTA Language:** Is the button text action-oriented (e.g., "Send My Template")?

## 4. Consumption & Delivery
- [ ] **Format Optimization:** Is the format the easiest way to consume this information (PDF vs. Video vs. Spreadsheet)?
- [ ] **Mobile Readiness:** Can a user easily view/use this on a mobile device?
- [ ] **Delivery Speed:** Does the "Success" page provide immediate access, or do they have to wait for an email?
- [ ] **Next Step:** Does the lead magnet itself include a clear CTA for the next step in the funnel?
