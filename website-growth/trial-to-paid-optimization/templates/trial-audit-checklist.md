# Trial-to-Paid Optimization Audit Checklist

Use this checklist to audit a SaaS trial or freemium conversion path.

## 1. Tracking & Segmentation
- [ ] **Aha! Moment Identified:** Is there a clear action that correlates with conversion?
- [ ] **Usage Segments:** Can we distinguish between "Power Users," "Casual Users," and "Inactive Users"?
- [ ] **Drop-off Points:** Do we know exactly which day/step users most frequently abandon the trial?
- [ ] **Conversion Attribution:** Are we tracking which specific email or in-app prompt triggered the upgrade?

## 2. In-App Experience
- [ ] **Contextual Prompts:** Do upgrade buttons appear exactly when a user hits a feature gate?
- [ ] **Countdown Urgency:** Is there a persistent (but not annoying) countdown timer in the final 20% of the trial?
- [ ] **Value Recaps:** Does the app show the user how much work they've done or value they've gained?
- [ ] **Feature Gating Clarity:** Is it obvious *why* a feature is locked and what the benefit of unlocking it is?
- [ ] **One-Click Path:** Can a user initiate the upgrade without leaving their current task?

## 3. Communication Sequence (Email/Mobile)
- [ ] **The "Day 1" Welcome:** Does the first email focus on the path to the "Aha! Moment"?
- [ ] **Proactive Warnings:** Are users warned at 7 days, 3 days, and 24 hours before expiration?
- [ ] **The "Benefit-Led" Subject Lines:** Do emails focus on results (e.g., "Keep your reports") vs. process (e.g., "Your trial is ending")?
- [ ] **The "Soft Landing" Notice:** Is there a Day-0 email explaining what happens to their data?
- [ ] **Post-Expiration Follow-up:** Is there a "Wait! Come back" offer sent 48 hours after expiration?

## 4. The Upgrade / Pricing Page
- [ ] **Pre-Selected Plan:** Is the most relevant plan for the user's usage level highlighted by default?
- [ ] **Loss Aversion Copy:** Does the headline emphasize what they will *keep*?
- [ ] **Risk Reversal:** Are there clear mentions of "Cancel anytime" or "Money-back guarantee"?
- [ ] **Plan Comparison:** If the user has exceeded limits (e.g., seat count), is the correct plan clearly marked?

## 5. Post-Trial Recovery (The "Save" Flow)
- [ ] **Extension Offer:** Do inactive users get offered more time in exchange for a feedback session?
- [ ] **"Lite" Plan Option:** Is there a lower-cost tier for users who love the tool but can't justify the Pro price?
- [ ] **Data Persistence:** Are users told their data will be saved for X days, reducing the "Fear of Deletion" panic?

---

### Audit Scoring
- **0-10:** High Friction. Significant revenue is being left on the table.
- **11-18:** Moderate. Basic flows are in place, but psychological triggers are missing.
- **19-25:** Optimized. Conversion rates should be in the top 20% for the industry.
