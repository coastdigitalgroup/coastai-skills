# Customer Case Study Audit Checklist

Use this checklist to evaluate whether a case study is optimized for conversion or is just a passive narrative.

## 1. Above-the-Fold & Scannability
- [ ] **The Headline:** Does it lead with a specific, quantifiable result?
- [ ] **Executive Summary:** Is there a bulleted list or "Impact Box" in the top 20% of the page?
- [ ] **Skim Test:** Can you understand the Problem, Solution, and Result by reading *only* the headlines?
- [ ] **Visual Hierarchy:** Are the most important numbers (ROI, time saved, growth %) the largest text on the page?

## 2. Narrative Flow (The PSR Framework)
- [ ] **The Problem:** Is the *cost of inaction* clearly defined?
- [ ] **The Solution:** Does it explain the "Unique Mechanism" (the specific way the product solved the problem) or just list features?
- [ ] **The Result:** Are there both quantifiable (data) and qualitative (quotes) results?
- [ ] **Relatability:** Is the customer's industry, size, and challenge clearly stated so similar prospects can self-identify?

## 3. Social Proof & Authority
- [ ] **Customer Identity:** Is the customer named (with a logo and photo) rather than being anonymous?
- [ ] **Verified Quotes:** Is the quote specific to the results achieved rather than just "They are great"?
- [ ] **Visual Proof:** Are there screenshots, videos, or "Before vs. After" data visualizations?

## 4. Pathing & Conversion
- [ ] **In-line CTAs:** Are there relevant links or buttons within the body of the case study?
- [ ] **Terminal CTA:** Is there a high-value offer at the very bottom (e.g., "Get a similar audit," "Start a trial")?
- [ ] **Logical Next Step:** Does the CTA match the intent of someone who just read this specific story?
- [ ] **Alternative Actions:** Is there a "Download as PDF" option for internal sharing?

## 5. Technical & UX
- [ ] **Mobile Performance:** Are the data charts and "Impact Boxes" readable on a mobile device?
- [ ] **Page Speed:** Do high-resolution customer photos or videos slow down the initial load?
- [ ] **Trust Signals:** Are there security badges or industry awards visible near the primary CTA?
