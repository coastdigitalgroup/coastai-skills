# Competitor Comparison Audit Checklist

Use this checklist to evaluate an existing comparison page or to guide the
creation of a new one.

## 1. The Strategy & Framing
- [ ] **Targeted Headline:** Does the headline speak to a specific pain point
  of the competitor’s product (e.g., "The Fast Alternative to [X]")?
- [ ] **Winning Dimensions:** Have you identified 3-5 key areas where you
  objectively provide more value to the target persona?
- [ ] **Defined Audience:** Is it clear who should choose you vs. who should
  stay with the competitor (e.g., "Best for Teams of 10-50")?

## 2. The Comparison Matrix (The Table)
- [ ] **Benefit-Driven Labels:** Are the "Features" labeled as benefits (e.g.,
  "Automatic Reporting" instead of "Reports API")?
- [ ] **Visual Weight:** Is your column visually emphasized (e.g., background
  color, border, or "Our Choice" badge)?
- [ ] **Scannable Icons:** Are you using clear, high-contrast icons (checkmarks,
  X's, or data points) rather than just text?
- [ ] **Top-Loading Value:** Are your 3 strongest differentiators in the top 3
  rows of the table?

## 3. Objection Handling & Neutralization
- [ ] **The "Missing Feature" Frame:** If the competitor has a feature you
  lack, have you framed it as "Unnecessary Complexity" or "Enterprise Bloat"?
- [ ] **Honest Limitations:** Do you admit where the competitor is a better
  fit (this builds immense trust)?
- [ ] **Pricing Transparency:** Is the price comparison clear, including "Hidden
  Costs" like setup fees or required add-ons?

## 4. The "Path of Least Resistance" (Switching)
- [ ] **Migration Proof:** Is there a visual or text-based explanation of how
  easy it is to move data?
- [ ] **Migration Support:** Do you offer a "Switching Concierge" or free
  migration service?
- [ ] **Risk Reversal:** Is there a "Switch & Save" offer or an extended trial
  for those leaving a competitor?

## 5. Trust & Social Proof
- [ ] **Switcher Testimonials:** Are there quotes from customers who
  specifically used the competitor’s product before switching?
- [ ] **Third-Party Validation:** Are there G2, Capterra, or TrustRadius badges
  showing you outrank the competitor in key categories?
- [ ] **Data Recency:** Is there a "Last Updated" date to show the comparison
  is current?

## 6. The Call to Action (CTA)
- [ ] **Contextual CTA:** Does the button text relate to the comparison (e.g.,
  "Compare Plans" or "Start Your Migration")?
- [ ] **Zero-Friction Entry:** Is the first step after the CTA extremely easy
  (e.g., no credit card required)?
