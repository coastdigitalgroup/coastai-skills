# Urgency and Scarcity Audit Checklist

Use this template to audit a landing page, pricing page, or checkout flow for
missed opportunities to drive immediate action.

## 1. High-Intent Page Analysis

- [ ] **Procrastination Check:** Is there a clear reason for the user to act
      *right now* instead of in 24 hours?
- [ ] **Bounce at CTA:** Are users hovering over the primary button but leaving
      the page?
- [ ] **Repeat Visits:** Do analytics show a high number of sessions-to-
      transaction?

## 2. Lever Selection (Urgency vs. Scarcity)

- [ ] **Offer-Driven (Urgency):** If there is a discount or bonus, is the
      expiration date/time clearly visible?
- [ ] **Stock-Driven (Scarcity):** If the product is physical or limited-seat,
      is the remaining quantity displayed?
- [ ] **Logic Match:** Does the lever match the product? (e.g., Don't use
      scarcity for an infinitely scalable digital PDF).

## 3. Implementation Audit

- [ ] **Authenticity:** Is the urgency signal based on real data? (No "Evergreen"
      countdowns that reset on refresh).
- [ ] **Specificity:** Are we using specific numbers (e.g., "3 items left")
      rather than vague terms (e.g., "Low Stock")?
- [ ] **Benefit Alignment:** Does the urgency provide a benefit? (e.g., "Order
      now for Friday delivery" vs. just "Hurry").
- [ ] **Visual Prominence:** Is the trigger placed near the CTA without
      cluttering the UI?

## 4. Ethical & Brand Alignment

- [ ] **Brand Integrity:** Does the urgency level match the brand voice? (Avoid
      "Used Car Salesman" vibes for premium brands).
- [ ] **Legal Compliance:** Are we being transparent about when an offer ends?
- [ ] **Pressure Check:** Does the UI allow the user to focus on the value
      before being hit with the urgency signal?

## 5. Mobile Optimization

- [ ] **Viewport Check:** Are countdown timers or stock badges visible without
      pushing the primary CTA below the fold?
- [ ] **Tap Targets:** Are any "Dismiss" or "X" buttons on urgency popups large
      enough (44x44px)?
