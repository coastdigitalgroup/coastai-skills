# Example: SaaS Explainer Video Optimization

## Scenario

**Company:** "CloudTask," a project management tool for creative agencies.
**Asset:** A 2-minute "How it Works" explainer video on the homepage.
**Problem:** The video had a 5% play rate and a massive 60% drop-off within the
first 10 seconds. Conversion from the homepage was stagnating.

## Before Optimization

- **Thumbnail:** A generic screenshot of the dashboard with a tiny "Play" icon.
- **Title:** "CloudTask Feature Overview."
- **Intro:** A 12-second animated logo sequence with upbeat corporate music.
- **Content:** The founder speaking over screenshots, explaining the history of
  why they built the tool.
- **CTA:** At the very end (1:55), the founder says, "Visit our website to
  learn more."

## The Optimization (The Skill Applied)

CloudTask applied the **Video Conversion Optimization** skill to overhaul the
packaging and the "First Mile" of the video.

1.  **Packaging Overhaul:**
    - **New Thumbnail:** A high-contrast shot of a project manager looking
      relieved, with bold text overlay: "Save 10 Hours/Week."
    - **New Title:** "How CloudTask Automates Your Agency’s Busywork."
2.  **The "Kill the Logo" Edit:**
    - Removed the 12-second logo intro. The video now starts with a split-screen
      showing a "Messy Spreadsheet" vs. "Clean CloudTask Dashboard."
3.  **The "Problem-First" Hook:**
    - The first 5 seconds now address the pain point: "Still managing agency
      deadlines with three different apps and a dozen spreadsheets? Stop."
4.  **In-Video Persuasion:**
    - Added text overlays highlighting key benefits (e.g., "One-click reporting")
      so the video is effective even with sound off.
5.  **Contextual CTA:**
    - Added a persistent "Start Free Trial" button in the corner of the video
      player (via an interactive overlay) and placed a large "Start Trial"
      button on the page immediately below the video.

## After Optimization (The Result)

- **Play Rate:** Increased from **5% to 18%** (Users were more curious about
  the "10 Hours/Week" promise).
- **Early Retention:** Drop-off in the first 10 seconds decreased from **60% to
  15%** (The "Problem-First" hook grabbed attention).
- **Conversion Rate:** Homepage-to-Trial conversion increased by **22%**, as
  more users actually consumed the value proposition and saw the CTA.
- **Watch Time:** Average watch time increased by 45 seconds.
