# Wishlist & "Save for Later" Audit Checklist

Use this checklist to evaluate the effectiveness of a website's wishlist
functionality and identify opportunities for conversion growth.

## 1. Access & Visibility
- [ ] **Discovery:** Is there a "Save" or "Wishlist" icon visible on product
  listing cards (PLPs)?
- [ ] **Proximity:** On the Product Detail Page (PDP), is the wishlist button
  within the "Buy Box" area (near the price and Add to Cart)?
- [ ] **Header Presence:** Is there a clear, recognizable wishlist icon (heart/
  star/bookmark) in the site's main header?
- [ ] **Cart Integration:** Does the cart page offer a "Save for Later" or
  "Move to Wishlist" option for each item?

## 2. Friction & User Flow
- [ ] **Guest Access:** Can an anonymous user save an item without being
  immediately forced to login or create an account?
- [ ] **The One-Click Rule:** Can an item be saved with a single click, without
  refreshing the page or opening a modal?
- [ ] **Visual Feedback:** Does the UI provide immediate confirmation (e.g.,
  icon changes color, a brief success message) when an item is saved?
- [ ] **Mobile Usability:** Are the "Save" icons large enough to be easily
  tapped on a mobile device (at least 44x44px)?

## 3. The Wishlist Experience
- [ ] **Call to Action:** Does every item in the wishlist have a prominent
  "Add to Cart" button?
- [ ] **Live Data:** Does the wishlist show real-time price changes,
  discounts, and stock status (e.g., "Only 3 left")?
- [ ] **Empty State:** If the wishlist is empty, does it provide helpful
  links (e.g., "View Best Sellers," "See Recently Viewed")?
- [ ] **Management:** Can users easily remove items or change variants (size/
  color) directly within the wishlist?

## 4. Re-engagement & Triggers
- [ ] **Price Drop Alerts:** Is there a mechanism to notify users (email/SMS/
  push) when a saved item goes on sale?
- [ ] **In-Stock Alerts:** Are users notified when an out-of-stock item in
  their list becomes available again?
- [ ] **Persistence:** Does the wishlist persist across browser sessions for at
  least 30 days (via cookies/local storage)?
- [ ] **Cross-Device Bridge:** Is there a clear prompt to "Sign in to save this
  list across devices" after 2-3 items are added?

## 5. Metrics to Track
- **Wishlist Add Rate (WAR):** % of visitors who add at least one item to a list.
- **Wishlist-to-Cart Rate:** % of wishlist items moved to the cart.
- **Return Visitor Conversion:** Conversion rate of users who return to the
  site via a wishlist-related link or notification.
- **Cart-to-Wishlist Rate:** % of items moved from the cart into the wishlist
  (indicator of reduced cart abandonment friction).
