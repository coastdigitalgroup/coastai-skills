# Interactive Demo Audit Checklist

Use this checklist to evaluate and optimize your self-guided product tours.

## 1. The Hook & Entry (The First 15% of the Tour)
- [ ] Does the demo start on a high-value screen (e.g., Dashboard, Results) rather than a Login or Home page?
- [ ] Is the "Hook" (the first action) available within 5 seconds of the demo loading?
- [ ] Is the value proposition of the tour clearly stated on the "Start" screen?
- [ ] Is the demo ungated at the start? (Highly recommended)

## 2. Narrative & Flow
- [ ] Does the tour follow a single, logical workflow (Problem -> Action -> Result)?
- [ ] Is the tour between 8 and 12 steps long?
- [ ] Does every step require a user interaction (Click, Type, Hover) rather than just clicking "Next"?
- [ ] If multiple personas exist, is there a branching "Choose your role" step early on?
- [ ] Are technical terms replaced with benefit-driven language?

## 3. Design & UX
- [ ] Is there a visible progress bar or "Step X of Y" indicator?
- [ ] Are tooltips positioned so they don't obscure the actual feature they are describing?
- [ ] Is the UI "cleaned up" (e.g., no confusing sidebar notifications or irrelevant data)?
- [ ] Does the demo load in under 3 seconds?
- [ ] Is there a clear "Exit" or "Close" button available at all times?

## 4. Psychological Triggers & Engagement
- [ ] **Endowment Effect:** Does the copy use "Your" and "You" (e.g., "Manage your team")?
- [ ] **Labor Illusion:** Are there brief "processing" states that show the product "working" for the user?
- [ ] **Peak-End Rule:** Is the final screen the most visually impressive or value-rich part of the tour?

## 5. Conversion & Lead Capture
- [ ] Is the lead form (gate) placed after the "Aha! Moment" (typically 60-70% through)?
- [ ] Does the lead form CTA offer a clear value exchange (e.g., "Save this work" vs. "Submit")?
- [ ] Is the final screen a clear CTA to the next step in the funnel (e.g., Book Demo)?
- [ ] Are you tracking "Demo Start," "Lead Captured," and "Demo Completed" as separate events?
