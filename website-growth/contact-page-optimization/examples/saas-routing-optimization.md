# Example: Intent-Based Routing for B2B SaaS

This example demonstrates how **CloudSync**, a mid-market SaaS platform, optimized their contact page to improve lead quality and reduce the support burden on their sales team.

## The Problem: The "Generic Inbox"
CloudSync's original contact page featured a single, long form with the header "Get in Touch."

### Before Optimization (The Audit)
- **The Form:** Name, Email, Company, Message.
- **The Result:** All inquiries (sales, support, billing, partnerships) went into one general inbox.
- **The Friction:**
  - Sales reps spent 30% of their day forwarding support tickets.
  - High-value "Request a Quote" inquiries were buried under "I forgot my password" requests.
  - Users had no expectation of when they would hear back.
  - **Conversion Rate (SQL):** 12% of total contact form submissions.
  - **Initial Response Time (IRT):** 24-48 hours.

## The Solution: The "Intent Hub"
CloudSync redesigned their contact page using the **Contact Page Optimization** framework.

### After Optimization (The Fix)
1. **The Intent Hub:** Replaced the single form with three high-contrast cards:
   - **Sales:** "Ready to scale? Talk to our growth team." (Primary CTA)
   - **Support:** "Need help? Access the Help Center or contact support."
   - **Other:** "Partnerships, Press, and General Inquiries."

2. **Branching Forms:**
   - **Sales Branch:** Added "Monthly Spend" and "Region" fields to automatically route leads to the correct AE. Added a "Book a Demo" link for users with >$5k monthly spend.
   - **Support Branch:** Included an "Account ID" field and a link to the Knowledge Base to encourage self-service.
   - **General Branch:** Kept to 3 fields for maximum ease.

3. **Trust & Transparency:**
   - Added a "Our Response Promise" badge: "Sales inquiries answered in < 4 hours."
   - Included photos of the Sales and Support team leads to humanize the brand.
   - Added the physical address of their SF and London offices.

4. **Optimized Success State:**
   - After a sales submission, the user was shown: "Thanks, Sarah! Mark from our Mid-Market team will reach out to you within 4 hours. In the meantime, check out our [Enterprise Migration Guide]."

## The Result: Measurable Growth
Three months after the implementation of the Intent Hub:

| Metric | Before | After | Change |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Form Completion Rate (FCR)** | 4.2% | 5.8% | **+38%** |
| **Sales Lead Quality (SQL%)** | 12% | 45% | **+275%** |
| **Initial Response Time (IRT)** | ~36h | 2.5h | **-93%** |
| **Support Noise in Sales Inbox** | 65% | < 5% | **-92%** |

**Key Takeaway:** By simply asking the user "What do you need?" and managing expectations, CloudSync increased their sales velocity and allowed their team to focus on high-value conversations.
