# Case Study Optimization Audit Checklist

Use this checklist to evaluate whether a case study is optimized for conversion
or if it is merely "marketing fluff."

## 1. The Result-First Headline
- [ ] Does the headline state a specific, measurable result?
- [ ] Does it name the customer or the specific industry?
- [ ] If someone ONLY reads the headline, do they understand the value?

## 2. The Summary (tl;dr) Section
- [ ] Is there a summary box or sidebar visible above the fold?
- [ ] Does it list the "North Star" metric?
- [ ] Does it specify which product/service was used?
- [ ] Is there a "Fast Facts" section (e.g., Company Size, Industry, Location)?

## 3. The PSR Structure (Narrative Flow)
- [ ] **Problem:** Is the pain point described in business terms (e.g., lost
      revenue, wasted time)?
- [ ] **Solution:** Does it explain *how* the product solved the problem without
      getting bogged down in too much technical detail?
- [ ] **Result:** Is the result quantified (%, $, or hours)?
- [ ] Is the transition between these three sections logical and clear?

## 4. Design & Scannability
- [ ] Are there "Pull Quotes" that are visually distinct from the body text?
- [ ] Do the pull quotes address a common objection or fear?
- [ ] Are there bolded subheadings that tell a story on their own?
- [ ] Are there visual aids (charts, screenshots, "before/after" photos)?

## 5. Trust & Relatability
- [ ] Is the customer's name, title, and headshot included?
- [ ] Is the company logo visible?
- [ ] Does the customer look and sound like your "Ideal Customer Profile" (ICP)?

## 6. Conversion Paths
- [ ] Is there an "Inline CTA" halfway through the content?
- [ ] Is there a primary CTA at the bottom of the page?
- [ ] Is the CTA benefit-driven (e.g., "Get the ROI Report") rather than generic
      ("Contact Us")?

---

## Scoring Table

| Score | Rating | Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **0-3** | **Narrative Fluff** | High risk of high bounce rate. Requires full PSR rewrite. |
| **4-6** | **Passive Proof** | Good story, weak impact. Add metrics and scannable elements. |
| **7-9** | **Growth Asset** | Strong conversion tool. A/B test CTAs for marginal gains. |
| **10+** | **High-Impact Hero** | Use as a "Top Performer" and promote in paid campaigns. |
