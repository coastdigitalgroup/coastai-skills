# Lead Magnet Types & Psychological Heuristics

Choosing the right format and psychological "hook" is critical for lead magnet performance.

## 1. Format Selection by Intent

| Format | Best For... | User Effort | Perceived Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Checklist / Cheat Sheet** | Speed and implementation. | Very Low | High (Utility) |
| **Templates / Calculators** | Solving a technical/math problem. | Low | Very High (Saves time) |
| **Ebooks / Whitepapers** | Establishing authority on complex topics. | High | Medium (Can be "fluff") |
| **Webinars / Videos** | Visual demonstration or training. | Medium | High (Engagement) |
| **Free Audit / Quiz** | Personalized feedback. | Medium | Extreme (Customization) |

## 2. Psychological Principles

### The Endorsement of Specificity
Users are more likely to convert for a "Specific Solution" than a "General Education."
- *Heuristic:* The more specific the headline, the higher the conversion, but potentially smaller the total audience.

### The Labor Illusion
Users value tools that appear to have done the "heavy lifting" for them.
- *Application:* Spreadsheet calculators or pre-written email templates perform well because they represent hours of work handed over for "free."

### The Zeigarnik Effect (The Open Loop)
Starting a process and needing to finish it.
- *Application:* Quizzes and assessments use this. The user invests 2 minutes answering questions and provides an email to "close the loop" and see their results.

### Reciprocity
When you give someone something valuable first, they feel a subconscious obligation to return the favor (often by continuing the relationship).

## 3. The "Cost" of Data (Friction Heuristics)

- **Email only:** ~25-50% conversion rate (on highly targeted traffic).
- **Email + Name:** ~15-30% conversion rate.
- **Email + Name + Company/Role:** ~5-15% conversion rate.
- **Phone Number:** Usually causes a 40-60% drop in conversion from the baseline.

**Decision Rule:** Only ask for data you will *immediately* use to personalize the delivery or qualify the lead. If you aren't going to call them in the next 24 hours, don't ask for a phone number on a lead magnet.
