# Case Study Optimization: Before & After

This example demonstrates the optimization of a B2B SaaS case study for a
fictional CRM tool, "DealFlow."

## Scenario

**Goal:** Increase the number of demo requests originating from the "Success
Stories" page.

**The Subject:** A case study about "Global Retail Corp" using DealFlow to manage
their sales pipeline.

---

## Before: The "Narrative-Heavy" Approach

### Headline: Global Retail Corp Partners with DealFlow for Success

**The Content:**
Global Retail Corp is a leader in the retail space, founded in 1985 with over
500 locations nationwide. For years, they struggled with manual data entry and a
lack of visibility into their sales process. Their sales team was using a mix of
spreadsheets and legacy software that didn't talk to each other.

When they found DealFlow, they were impressed by our interface. We worked
closely with their IT department for a six-month implementation period. Since
going live, the team has reported that they are much happier with the new
system. "It's just so much easier to use than what we had before," says John
Doe, Head of Sales.

**The Outcome:**
- Qualitative praise for the UI.
- No clear metrics.
- High bounce rate (72%) as users got bored by the company history.
- 0.5% CTR to the "Book a Demo" button at the very bottom.

---

## After: The "Result-First" PSR Approach

### Headline: How Global Retail Corp Shortened Their Sales Cycle by 30% with DealFlow

### Executive Summary (The tl;dr Box)
- **Primary Result:** 30% reduction in sales cycle time.
- **Secondary Win:** $1.2M in "lost leads" recovered in Q1.
- **Hero Product:** DealFlow Enterprise + Pipeline Automation.
- **Implementation Time:** 60 days (Full migration).

### The Problem
Global Retail Corp was losing $400k per month in "pipeline leakage." Leads were
stalling in the negotiation phase because sales reps lacked automated follow-up
reminders, and managers had zero visibility into deal velocity.

### The Solution: Pipeline Automation
DealFlow implemented a custom **Pipeline Automation** workflow.
1. **Automated Nurture:** Stalled deals now trigger an automated follow-up at 48
   hours.
2. **Velocity Dashboards:** Real-time visibility into which stages are causing
   the most friction.
3. **CRM Migration:** Consolidated three legacy spreadsheets into one unified
   source of truth.

### The Result
> "Before DealFlow, we were flying blind. Now, my team knows exactly which deals
> to prioritize. We’ve seen a **30% drop in sales cycle length** and a **15%
> lift in win rates** in just the first quarter."
> — **John Doe**, VP of Sales at Global Retail Corp
