# Wishlist Optimization: Before and After

This example demonstrates how a mid-market furniture retailer transformed their
static wishlist into a high-converting re-engagement tool.

## The Scenario

**Company:** "Urban Nest" (Mid-market Home Decor & Furniture)
**Problem:** High traffic on high-consideration items (sofas, dining tables) but
a high "abandonment" rate on the product page. Users were interested but weren't
ready to commit to a $2,000 purchase in a single session. Their existing
wishlist required an account and was buried in the user profile.

## Before: The "Cold Storage" Wishlist

*   **UI:** Small "Heart" icon only visible on the Product Detail Page (PDP).
*   **Friction:** Tapping the heart opened a "Login or Register" popup.
*   **Accessibility:** The "My Wishlist" link was hidden inside a dropdown menu
    under "My Account."
*   **Communication:** No emails were ever sent regarding wishlisted items.
*   **Outcome:**
    *   **Wishlist Completion Rate:** 1.2%
    *   **Wishlist-to-Sale Conversion:** 0.5%
    *   **Return Visitor Rate:** Low, as users forgot which items they liked.

## The Optimization (The "Active Intent" Model)

Urban Nest implemented the following changes:

1.  **Guest Saving:** Enabled "Guest Wishlists" using local storage. Users could
    now "Heart" an item instantly without a login popup.
2.  **PLP Integration:** Added the heart icon to the top-right corner of every
    product image in the search results and category grids.
3.  **Active Triggers:** Integrated the wishlist with their email platform to
    send "Price Drop" and "Back in Stock" alerts for saved items.
4.  **Cart "Safety Net":** Added a "Save for Later" link under the "Remove"
    button in the shopping cart.
5.  **Visibility:** Added a persistent "Heart" icon with a counter in the main
    site header (next to the Cart).

## After: The "Conversion Engine"

*   **UI:** High-visibility icons on both PLP and PDP with "Guest" support.
*   **Friction:** Zero friction for initial saving; account creation only
    prompted for "Save for 30+ days."
*   **Accessibility:** Immediate access via the header.
*   **Communication:** Weekly "Price Drop" alerts and "Still interested?"
    reminders.
*   **Measurable Outcome:**
    *   **Wishlist Completion Rate:** 8.4% (7x increase)
    *   **Wishlist-to-Sale Conversion:** 4.2% (8.4x increase)
    *   **Average Order Value (AOV):** 12% increase for users who engaged with
        the wishlist before buying.
    *   **Email CTR:** Wishlist-triggered emails saw a 45% click-through rate,
        the highest of any marketing flow.
