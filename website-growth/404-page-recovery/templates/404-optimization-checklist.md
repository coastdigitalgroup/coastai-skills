# 404 Page Recovery Audit & Optimization Checklist

Use this template to audit your current error handling and transform your 404 page into a growth asset.

## Phase 1: The Leak Audit
- [ ] **Check Google Search Console:** Navigate to "Indexing" > "Pages" > "Not found (404)". Identify the top 10 URLs with the most impressions.
- [ ] **Identify Referrer Sources:** Use Google Analytics (Behavior > Site Content > All Pages > filter for 404 page title) to see *where* users are coming from before hitting the 404.
- [ ] **Pattern Analysis:** Are the 404s concentrated in a specific subdirectory (e.g., `/products/` or `/blog/`)?

## Phase 2: User Experience Optimization
- [ ] **Headline Clarity:** Does the headline acknowledge the error without being overly technical?
- [ ] **Search Prominence:** Is there a search bar? Is it the most visually prominent element on the page?
- [ ] **Actionable Links:** Are there 3-5 links to high-value pages (e.g., Home, Best Sellers, Sale)?
- [ ] **Visual Consistency:** Does the page look like the rest of the site, or does it feel like a "broken" system page?
- [ ] **Mobile Usability:** Is the search bar and the primary links easy to tap on mobile without zooming?

## Phase 3: The "Plan B" Conversion
- [ ] **Dynamic Recommendations:** Can you show products or articles based on the broken URL's keywords?
- [ ] **The "Apology" Offer:** Is there a small incentive (discount, free resource) to keep the user from leaving?
- [ ] **Lead Capture:** If they can't find what they want, is there an option to "Join the waitlist" or "Get updates"?

## Phase 4: Technical & SEO Health
- [ ] **Status Code Check:** Use a tool like "Check My Links" or DevTools to ensure the page returns a true `404 Not Found` status (not a `200 OK` Soft 404).
- [ ] **Tracking:** Is "404 Page View" tracked as an event in your analytics?
- [ ] **Search Success:** Are you tracking "Search from 404" as a separate segment to see if users find what they need?

## Phase 5: Success Metrics (Post-Implementation)
- [ ] **Benchmark Bounce Rate:** Current % of users who leave the site from the 404 page.
- [ ] **Benchmark Recovery Rate:** Current % of users who click any link on the 404 page.
- [ ] **Goal:** Reduce Bounce Rate by 20% and increase Recovery Rate by 15%.
