# Case Study Optimization Checklist

Use this checklist to audit and refine existing customer case studies for maximum conversion impact.

## 1. Headline & Hook
- [ ] Does the headline mention a specific, measurable result? (e.g., "50% increase in X" vs "How we helped X")
- [ ] Is the most impressive data point visible "above the fold"?
- [ ] Is there a "Results at a Glance" summary or sidebar for skimmers?

## 2. Narrative Structure (The PSR Framework)
- [ ] **Problem:** Is the customer's initial pain point clearly defined and quantified?
- [ ] **Solution:** Does the study explain *how* the problem was solved, focusing on unique capability rather than just a feature list?
- [ ] **Result:** Are the outcomes backed by hard data (%, $, time saved)?
- [ ] **The "Hero" Check:** Is the customer portrayed as the hero who achieved success using your tool?

## 3. Social Proof & Credibility
- [ ] Is there a high-quality photo of the customer or their office?
- [ ] Does the testimonial quote mention specific business outcomes?
- [ ] Are the customer's full name, title, and company logo included?

## 4. Visual Scannability
- [ ] Are there descriptive H2 subheadlines that tell the story on their own?
- [ ] Are key phrases or metrics **bolded** for emphasis?
- [ ] Are charts, screenshots, or diagrams used to break up long blocks of text?

## 5. Contextual Pathing (CTAs)
- [ ] Is there a CTA placed immediately after the "Result" or "Solution" sections?
- [ ] Does the CTA copy relate to the specific story being told? (e.g., "See how it works for [Industry]")
- [ ] Is there a persistent or floating "Contact Us" or "Demo" button visible?

## 6. Relevance & Relatability
- [ ] Is the target industry/vertical clearly labeled (for search and filtering)?
- [ ] Does the study address the most common objections for this specific persona?
