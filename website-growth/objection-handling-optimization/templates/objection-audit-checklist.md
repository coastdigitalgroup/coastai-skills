# Objection Audit & Neutralization Checklist

Use this template to audit your high-conversion pages (Pricing, Checkout, Lead
Gen) and identify where "Fears, Uncertainties, and Doubts" (FUDs) are killing
your conversion rate.

## Phase 1: The Discovery (Mining the FUDs)

- [ ] **Chat/Support Review:** Search for questions starting with "Can I...",
  "Do you...", or "What if..." from the last 30 days.
- [ ] **Sales Team Interview:** Ask sales: "What are the 3 reasons prospects
  give for not moving forward after seeing the price?"
- [ ] **Survey Analysis:** Review "exit" or "abandonment" survey data for
  patterns related to trust, price, or complexity.
- [ ] **Competitor Comparison:** Look at competitor "Us vs Them" pages. What
  objections are they raising about *your* product?

## Phase 2: Mapping the Objections

For each key page, identify the top 3 objections:

| Page | Objection | Category | Severity (1-5) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Pricing | e.g., "Is there a long-term contract?" | Price/Value | 5 |
| Checkout | e.g., "When will it arrive?" | Logistics | 4 |
| Lead Gen | e.g., "Will you spam me?" | Trust/Safety | 4 |

## Phase 3: Drafting Neutralizers

Apply the correct "Neutralizer Type" to the objection:

- **Type A: Proof/Data** (Use for technical or security doubts)
- **Type B: Risk Reversal** (Use for price or commitment doubts)
- **Type C: Social Proof** (Use for ease-of-use or suitability doubts)
- **Type D: Empathy/Acknowledgment** (Use for complex transitions or pain
  points)

## Phase 4: Implementation (The Proximity Test)

- [ ] **Visual Field Check:** Is the neutralizer copy within 100px of the CTA
  or the element causing the doubt?
- [ ] **Scannability Check:** Can the objection be answered in under 5 seconds
  of reading?
- [ ] **Mobile Fit:** Does the added copy clutter the mobile view or hide the
  CTA?
- [ ] **Clarity Check:** Does the answer use plain language (no jargon)?

## Phase 5: Validation

- [ ] **Baseline Established:** Recorded conversion rate before the change.
- [ ] **Tracking Set Up:** Event tracking placed on the "Neutralizer" elements
  (if interactive).
- [ ] **A/B Test Ready:** (Optional) Version A (Original) vs Version B
  (With Objection Handling).
