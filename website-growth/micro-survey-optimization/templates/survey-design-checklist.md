# Micro-Survey Design & Audit Checklist

Use this checklist to plan a new micro-survey or audit an existing one to ensure
maximum response rates and insight quality.

## 1. The Strategy (The "Why")
- [ ] **Define the Insight Gap:** What is the one thing we don't know about user
  behavior on this page?
- [ ] **Goal-to-Metric Alignment:** How will the answers to this survey change
  our next A/B test?
- [ ] **Targeting:** Is this for all visitors, or a specific segment (e.g.,
  new vs. returning, mobile vs. desktop)?

## 2. The Trigger (The "When")
- [ ] **Exit Intent:** Triggered when the user is leaving (Best for "Why didn't
  you buy?").
- [ ] **Time on Page:** Triggered after X seconds (Best for "Did you find what
  you were looking for?").
- [ ] **Scroll Depth:** Triggered at X% scroll (Best for content-heavy pages).
- [ ] **Negative Filtering:** Have we ensured this *won't* show to someone who
  already converted or saw it in the last 30 days?

## 3. The Hook (The "How")
- [ ] **Clear H1:** Is the question specific and benefit-driven?
- [ ] **Micro-Choices:** Are there 3–5 pre-defined answers to choose from?
- [ ] **Scannability:** Can the user understand the question in < 2 seconds?
- [ ] **Tone:** Does the language match the page context (e.g., helpful and
  humble vs. urgent)?

## 4. The Logic (The "Flow")
- [ ] **Branching:** If they select a specific choice, do we ask a relevant
  follow-up?
- [ ] **The "Other" Option:** Is there an open-text box for unexpected answers?
- [ ] **Thank You State:** Does the survey close gracefully with a "Thank You"
  or a relevant resource?
- [ ] **The Email Ask:** If asking for an email, is it optional and placed at
  the very end?

## 5. Mobile & UX (The "Feel")
- [ ] **Viewport Check:** Does the survey block the primary CTA on mobile?
- [ ] **Tap Targets:** Are the buttons at least 44x44px?
- [ ] **Contrast:** Is the survey visually distinct from the page background?
- [ ] **Dismissibility:** Is there a clear, easy-to-hit "X" or "Close" button?

## 6. Post-Survey (The "Action")
- [ ] **Reporting:** Where does this data go? (Slack, CRM, Spreadsheet?)
- [ ] **Review Cadence:** Who is responsible for reviewing the data weekly?
- [ ] **Test Generation:** Is there a process for turning "Top Responses" into
  A/B test hypotheses?
