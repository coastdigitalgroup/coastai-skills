# Example: Guided Discovery Optimization

## Scenario: "GlowUp" Skincare (D2C E-commerce)

**The Problem:**
GlowUp has a catalog of 45 different serums, creams, and cleansers. Their
"Shop All" page has a high bounce rate (62%). Heatmaps show users scrolling
rapidly, looking at 10+ products, and exiting without clicking. Customer
support is flooded with "Which serum is right for me?" emails.

**The Initial Approach (Before):**
The brand added a "Skincare Quiz" link in the navigation. The quiz had 12
questions, including technical ones like "What is your skin's pH level?" and
"List the ingredients you are allergic to (text box)." The final step required
an email signup before showing results.
- **Quiz Completion Rate:** 18%
- **Conversion Rate from Quiz:** 2.1%

---

## The Optimization (After)

Applying the **Guided Discovery Optimization** skill, the team overhauled the
experience.

### 1. Backwards Design (Outcome Mapping)
The team mapped their 45 products into 5 "Routine Bundles" (Hydration,
Anti-Aging, Clear Skin, Brightening, Sensitive). Every quiz result would now
point to a specific bundle + 1 "Hero" product.

### 2. Conversational Architecture (The 5-Step Flow)
1. **The Hook:** "What's your #1 skin goal today?" (Hydration, Glow, Clear
   Pores, Less Wrinkles).
2. **The Environment:** "How does your skin feel by 2 PM?" (Oily, Tight/Dry,
   Just Right).
3. **The Lifestyle:** "How much time do you spend in the sun?" (Images of
   Beach, City, Office).
4. **The Personalizer:** "What's your current routine level?" (Minimalist vs.
   Skincare Junkie).
5. **The Progress:** Added a visible progress bar: "Finding your perfect match..."

### 3. Language Translation
- *Before:* "Do you have Sebaceous Filaments?"
- *After:* "Do you notice small black dots on your nose or chin?" (With photo).

### 4. Results Page Optimization
- **The "Why":** "We picked the 'Hydrate & Glow' bundle for you because you live
  in a dry climate and want to focus on brightness."
- **The Shortcut:** A single "Buy the Routine" button that adds all 3
  recommended products to the cart with one click.
- **Risk Reversal:** Added a "Skin-Happy Guarantee: Love it or it's on us"
  badge right below the button.

---

## Measurable Outcomes

| Metric | Before | After | Change |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Quiz Completion Rate** | 18% | 74% | **+311%** |
| **Conversion Rate (Quiz Users)** | 2.1% | 8.4% | **+300%** |
| **Average Order Value (AOV)** | $42.00 | $68.00 | **+62%** |
| **Support Tickets (Pre-purchase)** | 120/week | 35/week | **-71%** |

**Conclusion:** By reducing the technical burden on the user and providing a
personalized, bundle-based recommendation, GlowUp transformed a point of
friction into their highest-converting sales channel.
