# SaaS Trial-to-Paid Upgrade Scenarios

This example demonstrates how optimizing the trial expiration and upgrade path can significantly improve conversion rates for a B2B SaaS platform.

## The Scenario: "ProjectFlow" (Project Management Tool)

ProjectFlow offers a 14-day free trial of their "Pro" plan. After 14 days, users are locked out and must enter credit card details to continue.

### Before Optimization (The "Paywall Cliff")

**The Problem:**
*   **The Experience:** Users receive no notifications during the trial. On Day 15, they see a full-screen "Your trial has expired" modal with a "Buy Now" button.
*   **Friction:** High cognitive load. Users have forgotten what features were "Pro" and what were "Basic." The sudden lockout feels aggressive.
*   **Metric:** 4.2% Trial-to-Paid Conversion Rate.

**The "Before" UI Copy:**
> **Headline:** Your Trial is Over.
> **Subhead:** Enter your credit card details to regain access to your projects.
> **CTA:** [Buy Now]

---

### After Optimization (The "Momentum Bridge")

**The Solution:**
1.  **Persistent Status:** Added a small, non-intrusive banner at the top of the app: *"7 days left in your Pro trial. [Upgrade Now to save 20%]"*.
2.  **Contextual Trigger:** When a user uses an "Automation" (a Pro feature), a small toast notification appears: *"You just saved 5 minutes with Automations! Keep this feature forever by upgrading."*
3.  **The "Value Recap" Email (Day 13):** An email sent 2 days before expiry summarizing their usage: *"You've completed 45 tasks and used 12 automations this week. Don't lose your momentum."*
4.  **The "Loss Aversion" Paywall:** The expiration screen was redesigned to focus on what the user *has already built*.

**The "After" UI Copy:**
> **Headline:** Keep your momentum going, Sarah.
> **Subhead:** Your Pro trial ends in 24 hours. You've saved **4 hours** this week using Pro features. Upgrade now to ensure your 12 active automations keep running without interruption.
> **CTA:** [Keep My Pro Features]
> **Secondary:** [View Basic Plan (limitations apply)]

### The Result
*   **Increased Visibility:** Users were aware of the deadline well in advance.
*   **Positive Reinforcement:** Highlighting "time saved" justified the cost.
*   **Metric:** **6.8% Trial-to-Paid Conversion Rate (a 61% relative increase).**
*   **Qualitative Feedback:** Users reported feeling "supported" rather than "forced" into the purchase.
