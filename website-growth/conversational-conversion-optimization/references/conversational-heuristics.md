# Conversational UI & Growth Heuristics

These heuristics are based on the psychological and technical principles of "Conversational Marketing" and "Real-Time Sales."

## 1. The Proximity of Intent
Conversational interfaces are most effective when they are closest to the point of user hesitation.
- **Heuristic:** A bot on the homepage is for *navigation*; a bot on the pricing page is for *conversion*.
- **Action:** Prioritize optimization on pages where users are making a financial or commitment-based decision.

## 2. The 60-Second Rule
In live chat, the perceived quality of service drops exponentially after the first minute of silence.
- **Psychology:** Users equate "Response Time" with "Company Competence."
- **Heuristic:** If your team cannot respond in under 2 minutes, use an automated "First Response" bot to set expectations or gather initial data.

## 3. The Sunk Cost of Conversation
Users are more likely to provide their email address *after* they have already invested 2-3 clicks into a conversation.
- **Psychology:** Asking for an email up front is a "Barrier." Asking for it after providing a value-add is a "Requirement for Delivery."
- **Heuristic:** Place the lead capture step *after* at least two interactive exchanges (clicks or text entries).

## 4. The "Micro-Choice" Effect
Starting a conversation with a blank text box creates high cognitive load ("What do I say?").
- **Heuristic:** Always provide 2–3 "Quick Reply" buttons to start the dialogue. This turns an "Open-Ended Question" into a "Multiple Choice Question."

## 5. Radical Transparency
Users are frustrated by bots that pretend to be humans.
- **Heuristic:** Clearly label your bots (e.g., use a bot icon or a name like "Support Bot"). When a human joins the chat, explicitly announce it: *"Now joining: Sarah from the Sales Team."*

## 6. The Golden Hour of Lead Response
Research shows that responding to a lead within 5 minutes increases the chance of qualification by 21x compared to waiting 30 minutes.
- **Action:** Use real-time routing (Slack/Microsoft Teams alerts) to notify the sales team the moment a high-value prospect engages with the bot.

## 7. Conversational Scannability
Conversational messages should be shorter than standard web copy.
- **Heuristic:** Keep bot messages to 2–3 lines of text maximum. If you need to provide more info, use a link or a PDF attachment.
- **Visuals:** Use emojis sparingly but strategically to indicate tone and direct the eye toward buttons.
